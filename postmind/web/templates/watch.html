About the daemon

The watch daemon runs in the background and calls AI triage on each account's unread emails at the configured interval. Results are stored in the local database and shown in the Triage view.

Alternatively, run the CLI daemon directly:

mailtrim watch --interval 30

AI must be enabled in Settings for triage to classify emails.
